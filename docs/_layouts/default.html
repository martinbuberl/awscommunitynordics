Welcome to MENA Game Tech Day

AWS Game Tech Day MENA is an online event designed to bring the MENA game development ecosystem together to share ideas, exchange talks, and discuss how cloud technologies are helping game studios and industries of all sizes. If you are in the process of conceiving your game as an idea, or are interested in understanding player trends, or want to integrate a particular technology into your game, or a company looking to integrate game development into its products, AWS GameTech Day will help you acquire the knowledge and tools needed to test and validate your thinking and approach, and then take your game to the next level.

Gunnar Grosch

Developer Advocate, AWS

Building a real-time leaderboard with serverless on AWS

The games industry has been moving rapidly toward multiplayer and competitive games, and an important part to drive retention and stimulate competitive engagement is the game leaderboard. But building a leaderboard that provides both real-time and historical rankings and at the same time is able to perform well when your game takes off can be a challenge. In this session, we'll look at how to build a customizable real-time leaderboard that scales with your game and allows you to compare and rank players from your games across all platforms.

Get Into The Video Game Industry

Denis Dyack is the Founder and CEO of Apocalypse Studios. He has been making video games for decades and has an extensive list of fantastic games to his credit. His studio evangelizes using a cloud-first approach in game development and partners with AWS Game Tech to move the medium of the games industry forward. The impact of content and game communities on game development and success.

5:30 PM (GMT+3)

Simon Tadros

Crypto Community Leader, Game Developer

Lighting Talk: Blockchain, NFT and the Gaming Industry

The concept of gaming has changed drastically over the decades, it became more as an investment or profession. Decentralized gaming offers gamers what they need – an economy based on principles of transparency, immutability and fairness, eliminating the possibility of fraud and allowing the gamers to start earning in-game, rather than spending. Using bloackchain technology helps promote financial inclusion for game developers trying to monitize and publish games. Crypto gaming and NFT gaming are two different segments, as the first is more focused on spending cryptocurrencies to earn and transact with other players, while NFT gaming is focused on using native currencies for generating and collecting unique NFTs that can be used in-game for special actions or held and sold later on. In this lightning talk we will discuss why the gaming industry needs blockchain & the future of blockchain games.
